How It Works

Three simple pillars to make sustainable packaging completely self-funding.

1

Flat-Pack Origami

Containers ship flat to save kitchen storage space. Pre-scored guidelines ensure a perfect 5-second fold during dinner rushes.

2

Checkout Micro-Fee

A tiny, transparent fee (₹2 - ₹5) is added to the user checkout. This micro-transaction fully funds the box production.

3

Closed-Loop Impact

100% biodegradable and compostable paperboard structures with plant-based coatings protect your food and the environment.
